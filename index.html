About

A brief history of Startup Village.

  • Fall 2013

    Ideation

    The idea of a student lead community of entrepreneurs was brought to life by a few forward thinking students and professors at the ASU Polytechnic campus

  • Spring 2014

    A Community Created

    After many months of interviews and foundational work the first residents of Startup Village were selected.

  • Fall 2014

    Move in

    The first year of villagers move in! Elections are held to delegate tasks and village objectives are formed

  • Fall 2015

    Open House

    Startup Village hosts the first "open house" a night where anyone is welcome to join the village to discuss their startups or seek mentorship/advice.

EntreFront

Get Connected. Get Involved

EntreFront is a networking platform for entrepreneurial-minded students. We make it easy for Arizona State University students to Get Connected with others and to Get Involved in startups, organizations, and events.
We do this by providing you with a database of entrepreneurial-minded people in a central, easy to use platform, and provide tools that allow you to search for the right people, startups, organizations, and events that interest you.

Aurora

The next generation of LED Underglows

Aurora is the first ever long-board under glow that is not a kit but a easy to install product that is easy to install and is capable of producing any color imaginable. Aurora dynamically adjusts to rider movement by using an on board accelerometer that takes in the riders movement and producing light effects with that data. Imagine carving down a road at night and one side of your board changes colors based on the direction you are turning. Aurora is being worked on by both Casey Lawton and his lead engineer Jonathan Baron. Jonathan is a senior as Arizona State University studying electrical engineering. Casey is also a senior at Arizona State University studying Technology Entrepreneurship Management
